# About Alexander Pankraz

![Profile Picture]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/profile.jpg){:.profile-photo}

I’m working on enterprise-scale systems, with a strong focus on graph technologies.

I design and build connected data systems—where graphs, distributed architectures, and cloud platforms come together to make complex domains easier to understand and work with.

A big part of my work explores how graphs fit into modern AI systems, especially in building more contextual and explainable approaches like GraphRAG and structured retrieval.

I’m particularly interested in how graphs can move beyond niche use cases and become a core part of enterprise architecture.

## What I Spend My Time On

- Designing scalable, cloud-native systems on AWS

- Working with graph technologies like Neo4j and Memgraph

- Exploring knowledge graphs and graph-based data modeling

- Applying graph thinking to AI systems (GraphRAG, contextual retrieval, agentic workflows)

## How I Approach Things

- I favor pragmatic solutions over ideal ones—especially in enterprise settings where constraints are real and trade-offs matter.

- Graphs are not just a technology, they are a way of thinking about data: focusing on relationships, context, and meaning rather than just structure.

- I care a lot about clarity in architecture—building systems that are not only scalable, but also understandable and maintainable over time.

# Data Drift vs Semantical Drift
In the world of Labeled Property Graphs (LPGs), we often obsess over schema and definitions. But if you’re only watching your labels, you’re missing the real threat to your production models.

> The hard truth: Your schema can be perfectly intact while your graph's predictive power is quietly collapsing.

The difference between Data Drift and Semantic Drift is the difference between a system that fails today and a definition that evolves tomorrow. If you aren't monitoring the statistical properties of your edges and nodes in real-time, you aren't actually observing your graph.
